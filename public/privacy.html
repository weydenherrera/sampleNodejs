Personal Data Protection Statement 

At Jinjerjade, the protection of personal information of our writers and readers (collectively customers) is extremely important to us. As a provider of trusted information on insurers, the collection and use of the personal information of our customers is fundamental to the integrity of our platform.
  • We only collect personal data that we believe to be relevant and required to facilitate your participation on our platform.
  • We use your personal data to provide you with better services and products.
  • We will not disclose your personal data to any external organisation unless we (i) have your consent or (ii) are required by law or (iii) have previously informed you.
  • With your consent, we may pass your personal data to affiliates of Jinjerjade, as permitted by law.
  • We aim to keep your personal data on our records accurate and up-to-date.
  • We maintain strict security systems designed to prevent unauthorised access to your personal data by anyone outside our organisation.

We pledge to fully meet the requirements of Singapore Personal Data Protection Act in respect of the collection, use, retention and disclosure of personal information. Each Jinjerjade entity, employee and representative is bound by our commitment to protecting the privacy of personal information.

Personal data collected from customers through our website will only be used for feedback, promotional or marketing purposes if the customer has opted into this service.

Personal data is encrypted to ensure security during transmission and storage.

See our detailed Privacy Policy

Definitions 

“Data access request”, “data correction request”, “data subject”, “data user”, “direct marketing”, “matching procedures” and “personal data” used throughout this Notice shall have the meaning as defined in the Act.

For the purpose of this Notice:

“customers” shall mean data subjects and include (but not be limited to) visitors to Jinjerjade Ratings Site who submit (writers) or consult (readers) the ratings.

“Act” shall mean Personal Data Protection Act 2012.

Data Collection 

The information we learn from customers helps us personalize and continually improve our site and your experience. Here are the types of information we gather:
  1. We receive and store any information you enter on our Web site or give us in any other way. If you choose not to provide certain information, but then your access to functions on our site may be limited.
  2. We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. For example, like many Web sites, we use "cookies," and we obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses Jinjerjade.
  3. To help us make e-mails more useful and interesting, we often receive a confirmation when you open e-mail from Jinjerjade if your computer supports such capabilities. If you do not want to receive e-mail or other mail from us, please adjust your Customer Communication Preferences.
  4. We might receive information about you from other sources (e.g. social media) and add it to our account information.

Purposes 

The purposes for which personal data of a customer may be used will vary depending on the nature of the customer’s activities on the site. Such purposes may include the following:
  1. confirming your identity;
  2. confirming the authenticity of the information collected;
  3. researching, designing and improving products and services for customers,
  4. promoting products and services;
  5. making disclosures under and/or complying with the requirements of any law, rules, regulations, codes of practice, guidelines binding on or applicable to Jinjerjade including but not limited to disclosures to local or foreign regulators, governmental bodies, industry recognised bodies (whether within or outside Hong Kong) such as federations or associations of insurers;

Transferal 

Your personal data held by Jinjerjade will be kept confidential but Jinjerjade may transfer such data to the following persons and/or entities (whether within or outside Hong Kong) for any of the purposes set out in paragraph 2 above:
  1. any agent, contractor or third party service provider who provides administrative, telecommunications, computer, information technology, payment, data processing or storage, marketing, mailing, printing, telemarketing, customer satisfaction analysis, or other services to Jinjerjade in connection with the operation of its business;
  2. any person which has undertaken to Jinjerjade to keep such data confidential;
  3. any employee or associate of Jinjerjade;
  4. the insurance company that the writer has provided feedback on, if the writer has asked for the insurer to contact them;
  5. any person to whom Jinjerjade is under an obligation or otherwise required to make disclosure pursuant to any contractual or other commitment or arrangement with local or foreign regulators, governmental bodies, or industry recognised bodies (whether within or outside Hong Kong) that is assumed by or imposed on Jinjerjade by reason of its financial, commercial, business or other interests or activities in or related to the jurisdiction of the relevant local or foreign regulators, governmental bodies, industry recognised bodies.

Also, in the event that Jinjerjade or substantially all of its assets are acquired, customer information will be one of the transferred assets, but remains subject to the promises made in any pre-existing Privacy Notice and any opt out preferences expressed by the customer.

Use of Personal Data in Direct Marketing 

Jinjerjade intends to use, from time to time, your personal data in direct marketing of the following products and services:
  1. insurance, or other financial products and services;
  2. web-based services including but not limited to insurance needs assessment and product / provider selections;
  3. reward, loyalty or privilege programmes and related products and services;
  4. products and services of co-branding partners of Jinjerjade (the names of such co-branding partners can be found on our website, updated as necessary from time-to-time).

Only the following kinds of personal data may be used in such direct marketing or customer services:
  1. name;
  2. gender;
  3. date of birth;
  4. domestic setup;
  5. contact information (including but not limited to phone number, email address, social media id);
  6. information about the products and/or services the customer has purchased or applied for;
  7. information about the insurer and distribution channels (including their individual advisors or intermediaries) through which the products and/or services were purchased or applied for.

Jinjerjade may not use your data unless it has received your consent.

Data Subject’s Rights 

Under the Act, you have the right to:
  1. request access to your personal data;
  2. request correction of any of your personal data which is inaccurate;
  3. ascertain Jinjerjade’s policies and practices in relation to personal data;
  4. be informed of the kind of personal data held by Jinjerjade;
  5. be informed of the main purposes for which personal data held are or are to be used;
  6. make data access request and data correction request through the channel specified in below.

Data access or correction requests may be made in writing to the Customer Data Officer at:

Jinjerjade Pty Lte 80 Robinson Road #08-01 SINGAPORE 068898

In accordance with the provisions of the Act, Jinjerjade has the right to charge a reasonable fee for processing any data access (or correction) request.

If you are currently receiving direct marketing from Jinjerjade but do not wish Jinjerjade to continue to use their personal data for use for direct marketing, you may, without charge, change your Customer Communication preferences on-line, or they can contact Jinjerjade at optout@Jinjerjade.com

Use of Cookies 

Information about your visit to this website may be recorded for in order to improve the usability of this the website for you and other visitors. Some of this information will be gathered through the use of "cookies". Cookies are small bits of information that are automatically stored on a person's computer that can be retrieved by this website. Cookies can make the website more useful to you by storing information about your preferences and history, thus enabling Jinjerjade to provide more useful features for you. The data collected by "cookies" contain no personally identifiable information. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can set your browser to disable cookies or inform you when they are accepted. However, by disabling cookies, you may not be able to take full advantage of our website.

We use cookies to:
  1. allow our web server to determine whether the cookies setting on your web browser has been enabled or disabled so that we will know whether data can be collected from your web browser;
  2. track your visits to our website and recognize you when you are a repeat visitor so that we can gather statistics on the number of new and repeat visitors and evaluate website effectiveness;
  3. temporarily store information on your preferences and your path between pages on our website to give us insight on ways to further improve our website to provide a more useful, customized and enriching website experience;
  4. temporarily identify your web browser after you have logged in on a secured page on our website so that our web browser can maintain a dialogue with your web browser in order for you to carry out certain activities.

We may also use web analytics to research usage patterns and activities on our website.
